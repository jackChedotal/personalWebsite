Projects

College Microsite

This website was made for a college assignment, for the purpose of showing what I have learned in lesson. I had a brief from a 'client' which I then had to design and produce a website for them. SA Historic Tours requested having a way to showcase their tours that they provide and also a way for people to book one. It was made using the 960 box method that was taught in class. The booking form was done using javaScript, this provided a way for me to work out the total cost of the tour. Although on a live website this would be insecure, this was the only way to provide that requirement due to not knowing a server side language at the time.
